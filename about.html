Forgotify tears through Spotify in search of songs with zero plays,
playing only the songs that no one else has ever listened to.

 Wrong. According to data that Spotify released in October of last year,
80% of the 20-million-or-so songs on Spotify have been listened to at least once.
That means 20% of the songs have not. That's 4 million songs with zero plays.

One crazy thought: when you listen to a song on Forgotify,
it increases that song’s play count… meaning that,
if this thing is working correctly,
no one will ever hear it again.
Each experience becomes ephemeral.
